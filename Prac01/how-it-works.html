How It Works

Three Steps to Rotate Your Reads

The Carousell is engineered for zero friction. From shelf to new reader in under five minutes.

01

Step 01

Snap & Scan

Take a photo of your book's ISBN barcode. Our platform matches the metadata, title, cover art, and original retail value instantly — no manual entry required.

02

Step 02

Set Your Terms

You control the lifecycle. Toggle whether your book is available for Outright Sale, Semester Rental (set your monthly fee), or Multi-lateral Trade.

03

Step 03

Meet & Exchange

Filter by local neighborhood or university campus hub. Arrange a secure physical hand-off to exchange items, eliminating shipping costs entirely — or opt for shipping if preferred.
